Roof maintenance involves regular inspections, cleaning gutters, repairing damaged shingles, trimming overhanging trees, checking seals and flashing, and removing moss or algae to prevent damage and extend roof lifespan.

Roof repairs involve fixing leaks, replacing damaged or missing shingles, repairing flashing around vents and chimneys, sealing cracks, and addressing structural damage to restore roof integrity.

Roof installation involves laying a protective underlayment, installing shingles or other roofing materials, securing flashing around vents and chimneys, and ensuring proper ventilation and waterproofing for long-term durability.

Roof replacement involves removing the old roofing material, inspecting and repairing the underlying structure, installing new underlayment and ensuring proper flashing, ventilation, and waterproofing for a renewed roof.
